About Us



This project is developed by Students of Third year CSE Students Under the prestigious guidance of Our Mentor Prof. Avinash M Ingole sir of esteemed Bharati Vidyapeeth College of Engineering Lavale .



This project is developed to digitalize the Montly Draft System used by organisation and to replace old and hectic paperwork required by the project. In addition to this the project has following benefits.

  • Admin panel to Edit,Maintain and solve issues
  • User can edit,update and download data all in you favourite Excel file!
  • Easy and Simple User Interface designed to boost your productivity (Pro tip:- It's much simpler than working on paper or Excel )
  • Date filter to help you find previously stored data
  • One click download Final Monthly draft From HOD and admin panel!
  • Responsive Website (i.e You can work anywhere anytime, all you need is internet and a smarphone) COMING SOON!
